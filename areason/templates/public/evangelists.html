Evangelist Aaron Hillyard

Founder of A Reason for Living Ministries

Aaron received Christ as his Savior on December 29, 2001, after which he attended Pensacola Christian College where he received his Bachelor’s Degree in Evangelism. In 2009, he completed his Master’s Degree in Bible Exposition from Pensacola Theological Seminary. Aaron has had the privilege of pastoring two churches, one of which he planted in Williamson. He is currently working in Evangelism. His passion is to effectively communicate the gospel to the lost and to equip the saints for spiritual usefulness. He and his wife, Sara, have been married for 10 years, and they have six children.
